# Skill: /challenge [topic or belief]

- Step 1: Search vault for all notes, claims, and references related to [topic or domain]. If no argument, ask.
- Step 2: Find counter-evidence, historical contradictions, and shifts in thinking over time
- Step 3: Build the strongest possible case against the current stated belief — cite vault evidence
- Step 4: Do not soften the challenge. The goal is productive discomfort, not agreement.
- Output: Devil's advocate analysis with specific vault citations
