# Skill: /ideas

- Step 1: Full vault scan — use `mcp__obsidian__search_notes` broadly to pull from all domains
- Step 2: Identify active domains and knowledge clusters
- Step 3: Find cross-domain intersections — places where two unrelated areas share a structural pattern or unsolved problem
- Step 4: Generate 5–10 specific ideas at intersection points. Ideas must be specific (not "explore X more") — they should be actionable experiments or hypotheses.
- Output: Ideas list with the cross-domain reasoning behind each
