# Skill: /connect [A] [B]

- Step 1: Search vault for all content about [A] and all content about [B] separately
- Step 2: Look for: shared vocabulary, structural analogies, cited by the same sources, appear in the same notes, or link to a common third concept
- Step 3: Surface 3–5 non-obvious bridges. "Non-obvious" means: not just "they're both about technology" — it means a specific insight that connects them.
- Output: Bridge map with specific connections and why each matters
