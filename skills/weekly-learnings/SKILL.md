# Skill: /weekly-learnings

- Step 1: Search vault for Weekly Reviews folder and any notes created or modified in the last 7 days
- Step 2: Surface: patterns noticed, insights gained, notable wins, honest reflections, things worth sharing with the team
- Step 3: Draft a weekly email — not a status report (activity → impact), but a genuine sharing of what was learned. Reference Ghost Writer Context for voice.
- Step 4: Subject line should be specific and informative, not "Weekly Update"
- Output: Ready-to-edit email draft with a proposed subject line
