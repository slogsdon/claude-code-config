# Skill: /stranger

- Step 1: Read the vault as if encountering it cold — no prior assumptions. Read `Me/` folder, `Projects/`, `Concepts/`, `Context/Career History.md`.
- Step 2: Build a portrait: structure first (how does this person organize their thinking?), then values (what do their decisions reveal?), then projects (what are they actually building?), then gaps (what's conspicuously absent?).
- Step 3: Write in third person. Be honest. Mention what would surprise people and what confirms first impressions.
- Output: A structured portrait. End with one sentence: "The thing most people probably misread about this person is: ___"
