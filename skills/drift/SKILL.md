# Skill: /drift

- Step 1: Search vault for intention statements — "I want to", "I plan to", "I'm going to", "I should", "I need to". Also read Weekly Reviews folder.
- Step 2: Search vault for evidence of what actually happened over the last 30–60 days — project notes, weekly reviews, inbox notes
- Step 3: Compare stated intentions against actual behavior. Surface the gaps.
- Step 4: Identify avoidance patterns — things mentioned repeatedly as important but never acted on
- Output: Gap analysis with specific examples. Be direct. No softening.
