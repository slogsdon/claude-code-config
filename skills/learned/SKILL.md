# Skill: /learned [topic]

- Step 1: Search vault for notes related to [topic]. If no argument, scan for content from the last 30 days.
- Step 2: Mine for raw material — specific insights, honest observations, things that were harder than expected, things that worked
- Step 3: Generate THREE drafts:
  - **Short post** (~150 words): LinkedIn format, hook → insight → specific takeaway
  - **Personal essay** (~400 words): First-person, specific experience, honest, not universal
  - **Universal essay** (~400 words): Broader audience, draws lessons that transfer beyond the specific context
- Step 4: Apply Ghost Writer Context — credibility through honesty, paragraphs over lists, no hollow CTAs
- Output: All three drafts, clearly labeled
