# Skill: /bloom [question]

- Step 1: Search vault for all notes that activate around [question]
- Step 2: Score each note by relevance (high/medium/low) and note its connections to other relevant notes
- Step 3: Present as a relevance map — highest relevance first, with a brief note on WHY each is relevant
- Output: Ranked note list with relevance reasoning and link connections. The goal is to show the shape of what the vault knows about this question.
