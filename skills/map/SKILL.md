# Skill: /map

- Step 1: Full vault scan. Look at all folders, note counts, and inter-note links.
- Step 2: Identify: densely connected clusters, orphaned notes (no incoming links), dead ends (no outgoing links), over-linked hubs, under-developed areas
- Step 3: Describe the intellectual landscape — what's thriving, what's neglected, what's structurally weak
- Output: Topology report with specific recommendations for improving vault health. Include candidate notes to link, merge, or develop.
