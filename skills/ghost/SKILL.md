# Skill: /ghost [question or topic]

- Step 1: Use `mcp__obsidian__search_notes` to find notes tagged with personal reflections, opinions, and voice-heavy content. Also read `Me/How I Think.md`, `Me/Working With Me.md`, `Me/Values.md`, and `AI Prompts/Ghost Writer Context.md`.
- Step 2: Build a voice profile — vocabulary, sentence length, structural patterns, signature phrases, what Shane emphasizes and what he avoids
- Step 3: Answer the question [argument] in that voice
- Step 4: Score fidelity: does it sound like someone who has actually done this, or someone reading about it? Does it lead with the point? Is it honest about tradeoffs?
- Output: The response in Shane's voice, followed by a one-line fidelity note (e.g., "Voice fidelity: 8/10 — slightly more formal than typical")
